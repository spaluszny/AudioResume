POST PRODUCTION.

I had the privilege of collaborating with student directors and composers on two short films. As part of a team of audio engineers, I recorded on-site and refined the audio during post-production, ensuring high-quality sound that enhanced the storytelling experience. Through these projects, I also had the opportunity to record my own foley and began building my personal sound library.

Films

When Birds Are Afraid of Heights is a short film that explores the complexities of young relationships. Through the thoughtful use of sound and music, our audio team enhanced the story, evoking deep emotions in the audience. For this project, I contributed as an on-site recorder and was also involved in ADR (Automated Dialogue Replacement) and music recording, helping to bring the film's emotional depth to life.
