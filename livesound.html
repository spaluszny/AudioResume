    LIVE SOUND.

    During my time at the Jacobs School of Music, I recorded over 600 hours of live performances. This included 7 major ensembles, two of which I Head Engineered. Collaborated with production teams to design optimal audio setups, enhancing auditory experiences for both performers and audiences.Troubleshot audio issues and optimized setups within strict deadlines, ensuring seamless live event execution.

    Performances

    Back To The Future (Jacobs Live at the Movies) - Second Engineer

    Recorded IU Concert Orchestra as they played the Back To The Future score alongside the blockbuster film. Created patch sheet, recorded and mixed concert for archives.
