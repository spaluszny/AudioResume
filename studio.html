STUDIO WORK.

Spearheaded the recording, editing, and mixing of 6 high-quality multitrack recordings in Joshi Studio, utilizing an S6 console. I gained strong technical proficiency in studio environments, ensuring the delivery of professional-grade audio mixes. I learned proper stereo mic'ing techiniques, optimal studio set-ups, and how to create a comfortable atmosphere for musicians to perform.

Recordings

All My Friends By Mindfully Blind

Tracked, edited, and mixed this piece using an S6 digital console, delivering a polished soundscape for Bloomington-based band, Mindfully Blind.

Disgrace By Ad Astra

Crafted and refined the mix with precision on the S6 digital console, capturing the unique energy of local Bloomington band, Ad Astra.

Women in STEM By Herd Immunity

Engineered, edited, and mixed for Herd Immunity with an S6 console, tailoring a dynamic mix that embodies the spirit of Bloomington’s music scene.

Picture By Mindfully Blind

Orchestrated recording, editing, and mixing on the S6 digital console, enhancing Mindfully Blind’s unique sound with meticulous audio quality.

Incoherent By Mindfully Blind

Tracked, edited, and mixed on the S6 console, with an advanced upmix from stereo to 5.1 and Dolby Atmos for immersive audio experience.

Pressure By Shades of Purple

Engineered and mixed using an analog Audient 8024 console, capturing a warm, vintage feel. Now streaming on Spotify and Apple Music.
